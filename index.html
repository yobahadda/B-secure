About

Mission

Vision

The B-Secure club is the flagship of the National School of Applied Sciences of Khouribga in the fields of cybersecurity and intelligent network engineering. Founded by a group of students passionate about computer security, the club quickly emerged as a key player in raising awareness about cybersecurity within the school. Our goal is to train highly qualified security professionals and contribute to strengthening the digital resilience of our region. Join us in this exciting journey towards a safer and more robust cyberspace!
The mission of the B-Secure club is to educate, raise awareness, and train the next generation of experts in cybersecurity and intelligent network engineering. We are committed to providing a collaborative learning environment where ENSAK students can develop their technical skills and knowledge in cybersecurity. Furthermore, we strive to promote a culture of digital security within our school and community by organizing workshops, conferences, and awareness initiatives.
The B-Secure club embodies a bold vision for a safer digital future. Our vision is one where cyber threats are no longer a source of concern, where individuals and organizations can navigate the cyber space with confidence. We aspire to be a beacon of cybersecurity in our academic community, illuminating the path to understanding, preventing, and resolving the challenges of computer security.
